# Specification: Refine Player combat mechanics and Enemy AI interactions

## 1. Overview
This track focuses on polishing the core action-oriented combat feel for Wlademir. It includes enhancing the player's melee and evasion capabilities, refining enemy AI (specifically Mummies and Skeletons) to provide a fair but challenging experience, and ensuring these systems interact cleanly with the existing procedural dungeon and dynamic lighting.

## 2. Core Requirements
- **Player Movement & Evasion:** Ensure dashes and movement are responsive and reliable.
- **Melee Combat:** Refine hitboxes, frame data, and visual feedback for vampiric claws.
- **Enemy AI:** Improve pathfinding and attack telegraphing for standard enemies to support tactical evasion and action gameplay.
- **Resource Integration:** Ensure combat properly hooks into health and mana systems.
