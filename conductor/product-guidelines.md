# Product Guidelines

## Visual Aesthetic
- **Retro Pixel Art:** The game adheres to a strict 32x32 pixel art base, scaled to a 640x360 target resolution.
- **Clarity & Lighting:** Visual design prioritizes clear silhouettes that interact predictably with the dynamic 2D lighting engine, ensuring gameplay readability is never compromised by shadows.

## User Interface (UI)
- **Arcade/Classic UI:** Interfaces should be clear, immediate, and easily readable at a glance.
- **Visible Tracking:** Health bars, mana, timers, and critical stats should be readily visible on the HUD, avoiding the need to pause or dig through complex menus.

## Narrative Tone
- **Campy/Bizarre:** The narrative tone leans into an over-the-top, dramatic, and intentionally bizarre style. Characters, dialogue, and lore should embrace a slightly exaggerated, theatrical vampire theme rather than taking themselves too seriously.

## Accessibility
- **Input Remapping:** Full support for input remapping is prioritized to ensure players can customize their keyboard and controller layouts to their comfort.
